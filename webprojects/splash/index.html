Check Please lets you pay your bill without having to get the check from the waitstaff

Simply open up the app and pay. Check Please can automatically figure out where you're sitting and look up your order, so there's no need to inform the waitstaff ahead of time or set up any sort of account. Everything is handled for you.

How it works

Check Please uses Bluetooth technology to automatically identify where you're sitting, and integrates with the restaurant's Point of Sale software to look up your order and let you pay. Credit card information is encrypted and stored on your phone, so there's no need to register any sort of account.
